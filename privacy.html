TripPhoto Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-03

Data Collection

TripPhoto does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All photo data is processed entirely on your device.

Photo Library Access

TripPhoto requires access to your photo library to display and organize your trip photos. Photos are never uploaded or shared with any third party.

Location Data

TripPhoto reads GPS metadata embedded in your photos to display them on a map. This location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

TripPhoto uses Apple MapKit for map display. Map tile requests are handled by Apple's servers under Apple's privacy policy. No user-identifiable data is sent.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at aabce.apps.support@gmail.com.
